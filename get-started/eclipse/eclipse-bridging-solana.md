Transfer your Solana assets to Eclipse using the Hyperlane Nexus Bridge to start trading or providing liquidity.

### Using the Hyperlane Nexus Bridge

* Navigate to [https://www.usenexus.org](https://www.usenexus.org/)
* Click `Connect wallet`

   ![](/images/image_49.jpg)
* Select `Connect to a Solana compatible wallet`

   ![](/images/image_50.jpg)
* Click on `From`

   ![](/images/image_51.jpg)
* Select `Solana`

   ![](/images/image_52.jpg)
* Click on `To`

   ![](/images/image_53.jpg)
* Select `Solana`

   ![](/images/image_54.jpg)
* Select `Select Token`

   ![](/images/image_55.jpg)
* Choose the token you want to bridge

   ![](/images/image_56.jpg)
* Enter the amount you wish to bridge or click `Max`

    ![](/images/image_57.jpg)
* Enter the Eclipse address you wish to bridge to, or click `Self` to use the same wallet

    ![](/images/image_58.jpg)
* Click `Continue`

    ![](/images/image_59.jpg)
* Review the transaction details and if content select `Send to Eclipse`

    ![](/images/image_60.jpg)
* Once happy approve in your wallet
* Wait for the transaction to complete
* Congratulations you have now bridged Solana assets to Eclipse
