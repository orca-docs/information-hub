There are two ways to harvest pending yield in Orca v2, you can chose to harvest:

* A single position
* All your positions at once

Harvest a single position

Harvest all positions

### How to harvest pending yield from a single position.

* Navigate to your portfolio (https://www.orca.so/portfolio).
* Click on the ellipsis button (`...`) to the right of your position

   ![](/images/image_91.jpg)
* Select `Harvest Yield`.

   ![](/images/image_92.jpg)
* Review the details in your wallet, including payable network fees and approve.
* Harvest Complete! ðŸŽ‰

You can also harvest yield from a single position using the [Portfolio Management Sidebar](/liquidity/position-sidebar).

### How to harvest pending yield for all your positions

* Navigate to your portfolio (https://www.orca.so/portfolio)
* Click on the `Harvest All` button to the top right.

   ![](/images/image_93.jpg)
* Review the details in your wallet, including payable network fees and approve.
* Harvest Complete! ðŸŽ‰
