### What Stays the Same?

While Adaptive Fee Pools introduce a new way to earn, many core concepts remain unchanged:

* [Ticks and tick spacing](/liquidity/concepts/ticks-and-fees) still define where your liquidity lives.
* [Fee tiers](/liquidity/concepts/ticks-and-fees) still serve as the base fee, and LPs choose them when depositing.

* You still earn [fees](/liquidity/concepts/trading-fees) when trades occur within your active tick range.
* The pool UI and deposit experience remains intuitive and familiar.

In short: Adaptive Fee Pools feel just like the Orca LP experience you know, but smarter.
