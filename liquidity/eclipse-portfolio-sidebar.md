The Portfolio Management Sidebar incorporates many portfolio management functions in one place, allowing you to quickly monitor and manage your positions. The sidebar can be opened in two ways:

* Navigate tohttps://www.orca.so/portfolio.
* Click on the ellipsis button ( ... ) to the right of your position

* Select `Position Details`, `+ Deposit Liquidity`, or `- Withdraw Liquidity`.

   ![](/images/image_185.jpg)

*Or*

* Navigate tohttps://www.orca.so/portfolio.
* Click somewhere on the line representing your position.

   ![](/images/image_186.jpg)

Once the sidebar is open you will see that there are three tabs:

* `Details`
* `Deposit`
* `Withdraw`

   ![](/images/image_187.jpg)

Besides a wealth of information about your position and its associated addresses, the *Details tab* also includes the option to *harvest yield*, for the selected position:

* Select `Harvest Yield`.

   ![](/images/image_188.jpg)
* Review the details in your wallet, including payable network fees and approve.
* Harvest Complete! ðŸŽ‰.
