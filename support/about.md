[Orca](https://www.orca.so) is the most trusted DEX on Solana, built by DeFi OGs for the best trading and liquidity provision experience.

<Info>
Orca is built around a Concentrated Liquidity AMM (CLMM) with powerful features for traders, liquidity providers, token creators, and builders.
</Info>

---

## Our Mission

Orca exists to change the way assets are traded through a paradigm shift in how market participants provide liquidity.

We strive to:
- Make trading more **efficient and transparent**
- Expand the scope of **what can be traded**
- Connect people across the globe to **coordinate capital** in new ways

## History

<Steps>
  <Step title="2021: Humble Beginnings">
    Two builders hacking at a desk in Tokyo, with no venture funding or famous backers. Launched one of Solana's first Constant Product AMMs (CPMM).
  </Step>
  <Step title="2022: Whirlpools Launch">
    Transitioned to concentrated liquidity with the Whirlpool program. LPs could now concentrate liquidity in specific price ranges for higher efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2024: Orca v2 UI">
    Major UI overhaul serving four user groups: traders, liquidity providers, token creators, and builders.
  </Step>
</Steps>
