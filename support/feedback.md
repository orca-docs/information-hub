## What Happens with Your Feedback

1. **Form submissions** are reviewed by the docs team and posted to our internal triage channel
2. **Page ratings** are reviewed weekly to identify underperforming pages
3. Patterns and gaps feed into broader content planning with Product, BD, and Marketing
